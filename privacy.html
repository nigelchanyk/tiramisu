Privacy Policy

Last updated: February 25, 2015

Please read the following privacy policy carefully before using the Lavendar iOS app ("Lavendar") operated by Team Lavendar("we", "our", "us").

Personal information collected

We collect your email address when you register for a Lavendar account. All information you entered while creating an event, including event name, description, date, time, and location, will be collected. We also periodically collect your current location when departure time computation is required. Note that we do not permanently store your current location. Once the departure time has been computed, all information regarding your current location is deleted from our system.

Purposes of personal information collected

Email address

Your email address is used as an identification during authentication. We may send emails to your email address if password reset has been requested. We may also send you information regarding any changes to Lavendar's terms of use or privacy policy.

Event information

All information regarding an event is collected so that you can review information regarding an event at any time. Event description is optional, and you may choose not to disclose any information regarding the event. We use the provided event date and time to send you a notification at the right time. We also use the time and location of the event to determine the appropriate departure time from your current location.

Your location

When there is an upcoming event, we periodically collect your current location to determine the appropriate departure time from your current location to the upcoming event.

Personal information protection

Security measures are implemented to ensure the confidentiality of your personal information. All information sent between the Lavendar iOS app and our server is exchanged via HTTPS.

Personal information stored on your iOS device

To optimize the performance of Lavendar, we cache event information of upcoming events and departure times on your iOS device. We also store your email address and login session on your device so that you do not lose your session when the Lavendar iOS app becomes inactive.

Disclosure of personal information to third parties

We send location information, including your current location and the upcoming event location to direction services, namely Google's Directions API and Apple's MapKit, in order to determine the departure time from your current location to your upcoming event. The request is anonymous and we do not disclose any other information about you to any direction services.

Your consent

By using Lavendar, you consent to our latest privacy policy.

Privacy policy update

In the future, if we update our privacy policy, the changes will be applied to this page. You will also receive an email regarding any privacy policy updates.

Contact us

If you have any inquires regarding Lavendar's privacy policy, please contact us at projectlavendar@gmail.com.
